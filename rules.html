Property Rules

Guest Profile

Family and Bachelors allowed.

ID Proof Related:

  • Local IDs not allowed
  • Passport, Aadhar, Driving License, and Govt. ID are accepted as ID proofs

Pet(s) Related

Pets are not allowed.

Important

Valid Govt Issued ID proof (bearing the holder's photograph and address) is required at the time of check-in for all guests.

Foreign nationals are required to present their Passport and valid Visa.

Indian nationals can present any one of these, which is mandatory:

  • Passport
  • Driving License
  • Voter ID card
  • Aadhar Card bearing the holder's photograph and address

Early Check-in and Late Checkouts

Subject to availability and to be confirmed with the hotel directly. Extra charges will apply as per the Hotel policy.

Storage Facilities

The hotel does not provide storage facilities other than rooms reserved by the guest and will not accept liability for any damage incurred to uncollected material - All things stored will be at the guests own risk.

Optional/Extra Services

The amount paid for the room does not include charges for optional/extra services and facilities (such as extra food and beverage charges, room service, mini bar, snacks, or telephone calls, etc.). These will be charged at the time of check-out.
